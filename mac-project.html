MAC Project Team Meeting #22

The Medical Assistant Consultant

A Software Requirements Specification (SRS) simulation

What is this?

For the 2019 winter semster at Centennial College the Software Engineering Methodologies 1 course assigned us a term project to create an SRS document for the creation of a hypothetical app. Highlights including having 3 weekly in person meetings totalling 4 hours a week with 25 logged meetings in total, an SRS document containing more than 50 pages in length, the utilization of Google Docs, Slides, Sheets and Microsoft Visio to organize and present our information, and a Discord server to coordinate project tasks.

The hypothetical MAC, i.e., no MAC app has actually or will ever be created, is a mobile application for the Android operating system, with a Windows desktop software variant, and is designed in mind for people concerned with various medical illnesses. The application will be able to find remedies to medical related problems a user seeks. It is also designed for people who wish to contact doctors for a medical opinion and or diagnosis fast and efficiently. Key functions of the MAC include the following: Live Chat, Self Diagnoses, Medical Checkup, Medical Tutorials, Health Monitoring, and A.I. Features.
